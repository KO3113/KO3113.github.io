More About Me

Ever since I was a kid, I had a passion for computers. It started with wanting to make my own content in the video games I loved. This dream of mine came to fruition when I discovered you could make your own Minecraft mods for the game. With my sister as my artist, thirteen year old me got busy following YouTube tutorials and learning java through Minecraft. The rewarding feeling I felt when I saw my content in the game was incredible. Now, a college senior at Shippensburg University, my love for computer science has only grown. I have learned python and C++ on my own, took multiple classes in Java and C, and got the opportunity to make myself an efficient and clean software developer. I have learned algorithms, databases, and networks. Each new topic draws me in and I cannot help but want to learn more. To this day, I still get that same feeling from my childhood when I solve a difficult problem or finish a project that I have been devoting days to. Entering the workforce will be yet another amazing opportunity to make me better at what I have been passionate about since I was young. I look forward to solving more problems, developing more software, and learning everything I can about computer science.
